### 29.3

In cases where the manufacturer offers a warranty period longer than 5 years, the manufacturer’s warranty will be valid for the period offered by the manufacturer and will be the manufacturer’s responsibility.

### 29.5

The warranty period for items purchased by companies or institutions, legal persons, is one (1) year.
